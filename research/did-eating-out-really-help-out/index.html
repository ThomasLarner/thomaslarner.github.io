Did Eating Out Really Help Out?

The impact of the Eat Out to Help Out Scheme on North Norfolk’s hospitality sector.

March 2022

I analysed the economic effects of the Eat Out to Help Out Scheme on North Norfolk's hospitality sector during the COVID19 pandemic. I presented my findings in the form of an extended essay, which I submitted to the International Baccalaureate.

What to expect: Analysis of the effects of the subsidy on eat out meals; changes in consumption patterns caused by the virus and the scheme; and the considerations and side effects of the scheme, to ultimately conclude whether or not the scheme provided a lifeline to businesses as a whole in North Norfolk.
